Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 13, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how our App collects, uses, and protects your information when you use it. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your data.

2. Information We Collect

Our App only collects the following information:

  • Bluetooth Data:Our App requires access to your device's Bluetooth functionality to connect with and communicate with Bluetooth devices.
  • Location Data: We collect location data to enable discovery and connectivity of Bluetooth devices. This is a technical requirement for Bluetooth functionality on most devices.
  • Diagnostic logs: When an App crashes or malfunctions, we may collect diagnostic information about the error and device configuration to help us improve the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

We only use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Enable Bluetooth connectivity within the App
  • Provides location services related to Bluetooth functionality
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues with your App
  • Improve Application performance and reliability

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Diagnostic Information: When you choose to send error logs, diagnostic information is emailed to our technical team for troubleshooting.
  • Legal Requirement: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to a valid request from public authorities.

5. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your information
  • The right to restrict or object to our processing of your information

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the feedback feature in the Application.
