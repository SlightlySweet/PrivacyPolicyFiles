Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your data.

2. Information We Collect

Our app collects only the following information:

  • Bluetooth Data: Our app requires access to your device's Bluetooth functionality to connect with and communicate with Bluetooth devices.
  • Location Data: We collect location data to enable Bluetooth device discovery and connection. This is a technical requirement for Bluetooth functionality on most devices.
  • Diagnostic Logs: In the event of an app crash or malfunction, our app may collect diagnostic information about the error and your device configuration to help us improve the app.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To enable Bluetooth connectivity features within the app
  • To provide location-based services related to Bluetooth functionality
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues when the app experiences problems
  • To improve the app's performance and reliability

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Diagnostic Information: When you choose to send error logs, diagnostic information is transmitted via email to our technical team for troubleshooting purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your information
  • The right to restrict or object to our processing of your information

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
